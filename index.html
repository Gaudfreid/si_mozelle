Room 02 — The Workbench

Clay, paracord, resin — and a lot of patience.

  1. Sketch the shape until it feels stubborn enough to be a character.
  2. Sculpt in polymer clay, by hand, in small batches.
  3. Cure, sand, and hand-paint every piece.
  4. Seal, attach hardware, and photograph it in the wild.
Room 03 — The Shelf

Small batches. Real scarcity.

Most pieces exist in a handful of colorways and never get remade the same way twice. What's on the shelf now won't be there forever.
